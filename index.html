about

Welcome! This is a personal site dedicated to archiving my digital art and other creative works.

A little more about me - I am an artist from the US with a love for character creation, world building, literature, technology, and music. My primary focus is digital art, however I also practice more “traditional” mediums such as acrylic painting or drawing with paper and pencil. Alongside my love for visual art, I also enjoy writing – specifically research papers, creative writing, and essays based on my personal insights. I plan on eventually using this site as a place to post my writing as well.

Thank you for visiting, for any inquiries feel free to contact me.
